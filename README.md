## 🏆 Flagship Projects & Innovations

### 🤖 1. AgriBot — Autonomous Field Management & Irrigation System
* **Overview:** A field-deployed, budget-optimized autonomous robot ($1,400 bill of materials) engineered to automate precision irrigation, soil fertilization, and crop monitoring[cite: 1].
* **Systems Integration Role:** Led field user research to diagnose regional agricultural hurdles, managed component sourcing, secured municipal endorsement and funding from Beit Liqya Municipality, and co-designed the hardware architecture interfacing 6-in-1 soil sensors over the **RS485 industrial protocol** with a **Raspberry Pi 4** host under the mentorship of Eng. Rwayda Assi[cite: 1].
* **Field Metrics & Impact:** Initial testing across **10 dunams** proved a **45% reduction in irrigation water waste** alongside an approximate **30% boost in crop yield**[cite: 1].
* **Global Accolade:** Secured **25th Place globally** at the World Robot Olympiad (**WRO**) in Singapore, capturing the **first-ever Silver Medal for Palestine**[cite: 2].

### 🪐 2. Astro-Tower AI — Autonomous Space Farming System
* **Architecture:** A closed-loop, edge-computed vertical farming system designed to sustain food security in extreme extraterrestrial environments[cite: 1].
* **Technical Integration:** Spectral data processing powered by an `NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano` combined with Near-Infrared (`NIR Sensors`) to evaluate plant health dynamically without human intervention[cite: 1].
* **Status:** Qualified for AlNayzak's **Tech-Talent** program and officially submitted to the international **AI4EDU Awards 2026** in partnership with **UNESCO** (Currently under evaluation)[cite: 1, 2].

### 🏙️ 3. Smart City Model — Intelligent Traffic Infrastructure
* **Concept:** An automated smart city traffic grid designed using **Arduino** microcontrollers to handle adaptive vehicle routing and real-time transit management[cite: 2].
* **Recognition:** Awarded **1st Place** in the regional **STEM Innovation Competition** and presented during the Demo Day organized by **Flow Accelerator** following the intensive City Challenge bootcamp[cite: 2].

### 📱 4. Faz'a Platform — On-Demand Technical Logistics
